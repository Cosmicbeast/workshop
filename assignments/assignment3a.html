About Me

Whether it's building websites, analyzing data, or automating workflows, I find joy in turning ideas into working solutions.

I’m always open to learning, sharing, and growing alongside like-minded individuals. This page is just a small window into who I am—a thinker, a builder, and a lifelong learner.
